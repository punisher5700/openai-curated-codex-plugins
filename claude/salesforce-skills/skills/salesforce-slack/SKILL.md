# Salesforce Slack

Design Slack-connected Salesforce experiences that improve actionability without turning chat into an unreliable system of record.

## Focus

- Salesforce-triggered Slack workflows and notifications
- Conversational approvals and collaboration flows
- Action handoff from chat into Salesforce records and processes
- Event-driven integration patterns and operational controls

## Rules

- Keep Slack as an interaction surface, not the system of record.
- Design explicit traceability back to Salesforce records and actions.
- Avoid brittle chat-only process steps without durable system state.

## Required Output

1. Interaction model
2. Trigger and event model
3. Record traceability
4. Security and operational considerations
