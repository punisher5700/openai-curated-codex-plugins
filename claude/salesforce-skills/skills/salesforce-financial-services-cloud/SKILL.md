# Salesforce Financial Services Cloud

Design financial-services workflows around relationship depth, servicing quality, compliance, and operational clarity.

## Focus

- Household and relationship modeling
- Client onboarding and servicing flows
- Financial account and interaction context
- Advisor, banker, or service-team operating model
- Compliance-aware process design and traceability

## Rules

- Model relationship hierarchy and householding intentionally.
- Preserve clear traceability for compliance-sensitive actions.
- Distinguish advisory workflows from transactional servicing workflows.

## Required Output

1. Relationship and household model
2. Servicing workflow design
3. Compliance and audit considerations
4. Integration boundaries
5. Operating model notes
