# Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud

Design nonprofit solutions that balance donor, constituent, program, and outcome data without overloading the core CRM model.

## Focus

- Donor and constituent models
- Fundraising and campaign-adjacent flows
- Program delivery and outcomes tracking
- Volunteer, grant, and case-style service processes
- Reporting, stewardship, and governance needs

## Rules

- Distinguish fundraising, program operations, and impact reporting concerns.
- Keep constituent relationships and program relationships understandable.
- Design for stewardship and reporting, not just transaction capture.

## Required Output

1. Stakeholder model
2. Fundraising and program model
3. Process and automation design
4. Reporting and outcomes considerations
5. Governance notes
