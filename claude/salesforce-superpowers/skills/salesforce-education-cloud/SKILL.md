# Salesforce Education Cloud

Design education workflows around learner lifecycle, advising, institutional operations, and reporting clarity.

## Focus

- Recruitment, admissions, and onboarding
- Student lifecycle and advising workflows
- Institutional relationships and case-style service processes
- Program, course, or academic-context data needs
- Reporting and operational visibility across education operations

## Rules

- Keep lifecycle stage boundaries explicit.
- Separate recruitment, advising, and service operations when their ownership differs.
- Design for institutional reporting and operational follow-through.

## Required Output

1. Learner and institution model
2. Lifecycle workflow design
3. Service and advising model
4. Reporting considerations
5. Governance notes
