# Salesforce CPQ

Design quoting systems that stay understandable under real pricing, renewal, and bundle complexity.

## Focus

- Product catalog, bundles, options, and configuration rules
- Pricing methods, discounts, price rules, and calculator behavior
- Quote lifecycle, approvals, amendments, renewals, and contractions
- Quote-to-order boundaries and downstream handoff
- Performance, operability, and admin maintainability in pricing-heavy orgs

## Rules

- Keep product and pricing models explainable to admins and operators.
- Separate pricing logic from approval and fulfillment concerns.
- Call out renewal and amendment impact early; that is where many CPQ designs break down.
- Minimize brittle rule overlap and hidden pricing side effects.

## Required Output

1. Product and bundle model
2. Pricing model
3. Quote lifecycle design
4. Approval design
5. Amendment and renewal strategy
6. Operational risks and controls
