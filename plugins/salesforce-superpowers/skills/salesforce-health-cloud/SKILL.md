# Salesforce Health Cloud

Design healthcare-adjacent solutions that respect care coordination, consent, interoperability, and sensitive-data boundaries.

## Focus

- Patient, member, provider, and care-team context
- Care coordination and service workflows
- Consent, privacy, and sensitive-data handling
- Interoperability and external health-system integration
- Operational visibility and longitudinal relationship context

## Rules

- Treat privacy and consent as first-order constraints.
- Be explicit about health-system versus CRM ownership boundaries.
- Design workflows that support operational care teams, not just record storage.

## Required Output

1. Care or service model
2. Access and consent model
3. Integration boundaries
4. Workflow design
5. Governance and compliance considerations
