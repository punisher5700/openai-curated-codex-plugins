# Salesforce Field Service

Design field operations that balance scheduling quality, dispatcher control, technician usability, and downstream service data integrity.

## Focus

- Work order, work type, service appointment, and asset relationships
- Scheduling and dispatch patterns
- Technician mobile experience and offline implications
- Inventory, parts, service history, and follow-up work
- Optimization, travel, territory, and calendar constraints
- Integration with CRM, ERP, and customer communication flows

## Rules

- Model service operations around real dispatch and technician workflows, not just object relationships.
- Be explicit about mobile and offline assumptions.
- Call out where optimization logic conflicts with operational exceptions.
- Keep service history and asset truth coherent across systems.

## Required Output

1. Service domain model
2. Scheduling and dispatch design
3. Mobile and technician workflow
4. Integration boundaries
5. Operational risks and controls
