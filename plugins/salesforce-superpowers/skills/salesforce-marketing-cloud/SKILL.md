# Salesforce Marketing Cloud

Design engagement systems that are consent-aware, segmentable, and integrated cleanly with CRM and external data.

## Focus

- Journey design and triggered communications
- Audience design, segmentation, and data extensions
- Consent, suppression, and compliance-aware messaging
- CRM synchronization and operational boundaries
- Personalization and lifecycle orchestration
- Failure handling for delivery and event-driven marketing flows

## Rules

- Treat consent and suppression as core design constraints.
- Be explicit about which system owns audience truth.
- Avoid pushing operational CRM transaction logic into engagement tooling.
- Separate campaign orchestration from identity and source-of-truth concerns.

## Required Output

1. Audience and consent model
2. Journey or campaign structure
3. CRM/data integration model
4. Delivery and failure considerations
5. Governance and measurement notes
